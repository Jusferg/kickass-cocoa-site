About Kick-Ass Cocoa

A community where women grow boldly, lead confidently, and build meaningful impact together.

Our Story

Kick-Ass Cocoa was created as a space for women who are ready to grow with intention, lead with confidence, and build lasting impact in every area of life. What began as a vision for connection has grown into a community centered on encouragement, accountability, and bold personal development.

We believe women deserve spaces where they can be honest about where they are, clear about where they want to go, and supported while doing the work to get there. Kick-Ass Cocoa is rooted in sisterhood, strategy, and the kind of conversations that move women forward.

This is a place to be sharpened, seen, and strengthened — not through perfection, but through presence, consistency, and community.

Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a powerful community of women who are equipped to lead boldly, grow continuously, and create meaningful legacy in their homes, careers, businesses, and communities.

We envision Kick-Ass Cocoa as more than a network — we see it as a movement of women committed to becoming whole, confident, and impactful together.

Our Statement

Kick-Ass Cocoa exists to inspire, connect, and empower women through intentional community, personal growth, and practical support. We are committed to creating an environment where women can develop confidence, strengthen leadership, and pursue purpose with clarity.

We value authenticity, accountability, wellness, growth, and sisterhood. When women are encouraged, resourced, and challenged in the right ways, they do not just succeed — they thrive.
