Why This Matters

Voice is more than sound. It is how your ideas arrive, how your confidence is felt, and how your presence is remembered. Strengthening your voice is about taking up space with clarity, intention, and self-trust.

Reflection

Where do I shrink my voice?

Think about where you hold back or soften your ideas.

When do I feel most powerful speaking?

Identify environments where your confidence expands.

What do I want people to feel when I speak?

Clarify your impact and intention.

Practice Exercise

Write a message expressing an idea, opinion, or request clearly and confidently.

Power Phrases

Instead of: “Sorry, I just wanted to add…”

Say: “I’d like to add a perspective here.”

Instead of: “This might be wrong…”

Say: “Here’s an approach worth considering.”

Try This This Week

Speak earlier in a meeting

Replace apology language with direct language

Practice one clear sentence of self-advocacy
